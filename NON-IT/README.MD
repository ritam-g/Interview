# Interview Questions and Answers

## 1. Why do you want to join our company?

Sir, I believe your company is a well-known company and provides good opportunities for freshers. I think I can learn professional skills, improve my communication skills, and gain real-world experience. I also believe I can grow both personally and professionally while working here.

---

## 2. Why should we hire you?

You should hire me because I am a disciplined person and a quick learner. I can adapt to new environments quickly and I always give my 100% effort to complete my responsibilities and achieve my goals.

---

## 3. What are your strengths?

My strengths are discipline, adaptability, and willingness to learn. I am a quick learner and I always try to improve myself. I stay focused on my goals and work consistently.

---

## 4. What is your weakness?

One of my weaknesses is that I pay a lot of attention to details. When I work on a project, especially the user interface, I want it to look as good as possible. Sometimes I spend extra time improving it because I want users to have a great experience.

---

## 5. How do you handle stress and pressure?

Whenever I feel stressed, I take a short walk and clear my mind. After that, I come back and break the work into smaller tasks. By solving one task at a time, I can stay calm and complete my work effectively.

---

## 6. Tell me about a problem you solved.

Recently, I was working on an MCP server project and faced an issue where the database was not connecting. I tried several solutions and checked my code multiple times, but the issue was still there. Later, I realized the problem was caused by my internet connection. Once the connection was restored, everything worked properly. This taught me the importance of checking all possible causes before assuming the issue is in the code.

---

## 7. Where do you see yourself in five years?

I have not planned everything for the next five years, but my goal is to become a better communicator and improve my leadership skills. I would also like to become a team leader and continue improving my problem-solving abilities.

---

## 8. Are you comfortable relocating?

Yes, sir. I am comfortable with relocation if it helps my career growth and supports the company's requirements.

---

## 9. What do you know about our company?

From my understanding, your company provides customer support and business services. It helps customers solve their problems and provides opportunities for freshers to learn and grow professionally.

---

## 10. How would you handle an angry customer?

First, I would listen carefully to the customer without interrupting. Then I would understand the issue and try to find a solution. If I need more time, I would politely ask the customer for some time and assure them that I am working on the issue. If I am unable to solve it myself, I would seek help from my senior and provide the customer with the correct solution as quickly as possible.

---

## 11. What motivates you?

Learning new things motivates me. I enjoy gaining new knowledge and improving my skills. Whenever I learn something new and apply it successfully, it gives me confidence and motivates me to keep growing.

---

## 12. Are you a team player?

Yes, I am a team player. I believe teamwork helps achieve better results. Recently, I participated in a hackathon where we worked together as a team. Everyone had different responsibilities, and by working together we completed the project successfully.

---

## 13. Why did you choose BCA?

I chose BCA because I was interested in computers and technology. After completing my 12th grade, I wanted to build a career in the IT field, and BCA was the best option to learn computer applications and software development.

---

## 14. Are you comfortable working night shifts?

Yes, sir. I am comfortable working rotational shifts or night shifts if required by the company.

---

## 15. Can you join immediately?

I have a planned personal commitment this month. After that, I will be available to join from next month and fully focus on my responsibilities.

---

## 16. Why are you applying for a non-technical role when you have a technical background?

Yes, I have a technical background, but I also want to gain professional experience and improve my communication, teamwork, and customer-handling skills. I believe this role will help me grow professionally and build a strong foundation for my career.

---

## 17. What are your hobbies?

My hobbies are learning new technologies, working on personal projects, improving my communication skills, and watching educational content.

---

## 18. What is your salary expectation?

As a fresher, my main focus is learning and gaining experience. I am comfortable with the salary package offered by the company according to industry standards.

---
